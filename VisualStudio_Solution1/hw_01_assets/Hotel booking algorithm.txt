Algorithm to book a hotel via booking.com

Precondition: there is internet connection.

1. Go to booking.com
2. Enter region where hotel will be booked, or hotel name.
3. Select trip duration.
4. Select person/s who will be stay in hotel and click "Search".
5. Filter request by required parameters (Your budget, Star rating, etc.).
6. Select the required hotel and click "See availability".
7. Select room type, number of rooms and click "I'll reserve".
8. Enter your details and click "Next: Final details".
9. Enter your address, payment details and click "Complete booking".

Postconditions: email from booking.com was received with booking confirmation and reservation details.
